At Indian Astronomy Rocket Research and Development (IARRD), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the personal information you provide when registering for our events and workshops, including "ஆழ்கடலில் ஒரு பயணம் — The Atlantis Protocol."

By registering for our masterclass, you agree to the practices described in this policy. Please read it carefully.

1. Information We Collect

When you register for our masterclass, we collect the following personal information:

  • Full Name — To generate your certificate of participation
  • Email Address — To send workshop details, links, and confirmations
  • WhatsApp Number — For updates, reminders, and support
  • College / School / Company Name — For audience insights and program improvement
  • Category (School / College / Professional / Enthusiast) — To tailor our communication

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To manage your registration and confirm your seat
  • To send session links, study materials, and event updates
  • To issue participation certificates upon completion
  • To notify you of future programs and marine tech events by IARRD
  • To improve our events based on participant demographics

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for commercial purposes.

3. Data Protection & Security

We take your data seriously. All information collected is stored securely using Google's infrastructure (Google Sheets) and is accessible only to authorised IARRD team members. We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss of your personal information.

Payment processing is handled entirely by Razorpay — IARRD does not store any card or UPI details. Razorpay is PCI-DSS compliant.

4. Your Rights & Opt-Out

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of future communications at any time

To stop receiving messages, simply reply STOP to any WhatsApp message, or unsubscribe via the link in our emails. For data deletion requests, contact us directly.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please reach out to us at:

📧 iarrd.official@gmail.com

We aim to respond to all enquiries within 2–3 business days.
